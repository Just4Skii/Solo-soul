Our Founder

Meet Hazel

Hi, I'm Hazel.

Solo Soul Experiences was born from my own journey through depression, healing and rediscovering myself.

I realised that many people spend their lives waiting—for love, friends or permission to start living. I created Solo Soul Experiences to inspire others to embrace solitude, build confidence and discover that joy can begin with a table for one, a sunrise walk or an adventure taken alone.

My hope is that every experience reminds you that you are enough, exactly as you are.
